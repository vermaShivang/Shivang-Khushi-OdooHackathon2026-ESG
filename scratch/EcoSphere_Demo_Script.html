EcoSphere fixes that by pulling Environmental, Social, and Governance data into one live dashboard, and making sustainability engaging through gamification.

Under the hood this is Next.js 16 with React 19, a 21-table PostgreSQL database, and Google Gemini AI. Notice I'm logging in with real credentials — authentication uses PBKDF2 password hashing with AES-256 encrypted session cookies, not a fake login."

Below is something extra we built — an Offset Simulator. Management can model 'what if we shift 60% to renewables' or 'cut travel by 25%', and watch the projected footprint and ESG score move in real time — a planning tool, not just a report."
